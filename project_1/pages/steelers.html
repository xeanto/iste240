The Steelers!

One of only 2 football franchises to win the Superbowl 6 times. The other is the New England Patriots. They are also one of only 6 franchises that don't have their own cheerleading squad. They played their first playoff game against the Oakland Raiders in 1972.

Odd tidbit; in 1943 they merged with the Eagles to become the Steagles.
